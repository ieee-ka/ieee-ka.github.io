We are excited to announce the **10th IEEE Signal Intelligence Challenge**!

This years motto is "Messages from the past", taking all participants on a journey through time. Messages from past challenges are mixed in with new ones.

In teams of 2-4 motivated students, you will compete to find the hidden messages transmitted by us, via radio but also through our matrix communications channel and this website. As in the last years, there are prizes waiting to be claimed! All info concerning the event is collected and published on our `Event-Page <http://www.ieee-ka.de/events/sigint-challenge/>`_.

You are already motivated and can't wait to start? Feel free to check out our `Presentation of the ISIC at GECON <https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.8163122>`_. There, you can get some insights into challenges from 2022 and already train your problem-solving skills.

TLDR; Kick-off Event: 23.04.2026, 18:00 at CEL (Seminarraum 2.OG, CEL, building 06.45 at Campus West). We will introduce you to the tools and techniques you can use to solve the challenges, especially help with capturing a real radio signal. We will also give you the chance to find your team members, so come to the Kick-Off even if you haven't found your team yet!
